How you can help existing mortgage customers

Here are some of the ways we can help you support existing NatWest mortgage customers:

Different ways to help and support

  • Product Transfers - Complete a Product Transfer and secure your customer a new deal within four months of their current deal ending. It's simple, straight-forward and requires no additional underwriting. 

  • Porting - Help your customers move home by exploring our Porting options. We will pay you a full procuration fee on the whole loan amount, not just the top up.

  • Additional Borrowing - Help your customers take new lending on their existing NatWest mortgage (subject to credit scoring and underwriter review). If eligible for a product transfer, an application can be completed to apply the new product to both new and existing borrowing.

Existing customer support available direct

To request a Redemption Statement, your customer can do this through their Mobile App or Online Banking. Full details can be found here.

If your customer wants to make any of the following  changes to their mortgage they’ll need to contact us directly:  

  • Change to their Mortgage Term. 
  • Change to their Repayment Method.
  • Transfer of Title - i.e. adding or removing a party from the mortgage.
  • 'Additional borrowing on a Buy to Let mortgage.

To make any of these changes your customer can call us directly: 0345 302 0190 (Relay UK: 18001 0345 302 0190) Monday to Friday 8am – 6pm, Saturday 9am – 4pm.

Manage your mortgage

Alternatively, if your customer has an existing mortgage with us, they’ll be able to make the following changes to their mortgage online they have access to their existing via Manage your mortgage, where they can:

  • Overpay up to 20% of the mortgage balance, through lump-sums or regular overpayments.

  • Change their payment date or payment bank account.

  • Request consent to let for their property. 

  • View their mortgage balance.

  • View their property’s Energy Performance Rating (EPC).

Payment difficulties

We understand that sometimes people struggle financially. If your customer is worried about paying their mortgage  it’s important they contact us as soon as they can.  

The sooner they talk to us,the sooner we can help and talk to them about the options available. 

We have a range of information, support and tools available for us for customers:

  • Manage your money.
  • Customers struggling financially.
  • Mortgage cost of living support.

Customers can also find further help by visiting struggling to pay my mortgage.

Customers in vulnerable situations

Customers in vulnerable situations are defined by the FCA as someone who, due to their personal circumstances, is especially susceptible to harm, particularly when a firm is not acting with appropriate levels of care.
