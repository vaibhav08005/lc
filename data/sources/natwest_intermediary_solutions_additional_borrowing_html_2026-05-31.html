Additional Borrowing policies and processes

ONLY FOR USE BY MORTGAGE INTERMEDIARIES

We do additional borrowing for customers who want to borrow more on their existing mortgage for an agreed purpose such as home improvements.

Why choose NatWest?

NatWest existing mortgage customers may be able to do additional borrowing. It’s a quick and easy process.  The new borrowing will be secured against their home.  Longer terms are available which could be more affordable for many customers. If your customers existing mortgage deal is expiring/expired they can apply to have their current mortgage and the new borrowing secured under one mortgage deal making it easier to manage one payment. 

Additional borrowing eligibility

 

Additional Borrowing is available through intermediaries either as a stand alone application or as part of a residential Product Transfer. If a Product Transfer is included then at least one sub-account must be eligible to switch. Our Product Transfer window is 4 months.

  • The customer must be resident in the UK.
  • They must have made their first mortgage payment. For Shared Ownership/Co-ownership they must have made their first 6 mortgage payments.
  • They must be up to date with their current mortgage payments.
  • Additional borrowing is available between £10,000 and £500,000.
  • Maximum LTV (including the new borrowing) of 90%.
  • Where the customer is using the any funds for Debt Consolidation, maximum LTV: 80%.
  • Additional borrowing must be taken on a Capital & Interest Repayment.

 

The customer would need to contact us directly if they want to do additional borrowing in the following instances:

If your customer has:

  • A Buy-to-Let mortgage.
  • More than five eligible to switch sub accounts.
  • A guarantor on the mortgage.
  • Requested to add/remove a joint party.
  • A Family Backed mortgage.
  • More than 2 borrowers on the mortgage account.
  • Power of attorney on the mortgage.
  • Land registry restrictions.

Then please ask them to give us a call or visit us in branch.

Call us on: 0800 096 9527
Relay UK: 18001 0800 096 9527

 

Application Process

How to apply for additional borrowing:

  1. Follow the Broker Portal tile for the 'Existing Customer Portal'.
  2. Click on 'Additional borrowing' from the top bar to be taken into the application.
  3. If the customer is in the product transfer window you will be given options of how you'd like to apply. 

Our Lending Decision

Once you submit the application you’ll receive one of the following lending decision messages:

  • Decline: This can't be appealed. If your customer is applying for additional borrowing with a product transfer you will need to apply for the product transfer separately.
  • Refer or Accept: If you receive either of these messages you will be able to proceed to submit a full application with packaging for underwriter asssessment.  If the additional borrowing is declined by the underwriter but your customer wants to proceed with a standalone Product Transfer application, please call our Business Development Team who can assist you with this.

Next steps after submission

Once you have submitted and selected to lock your application, you'll have 21 days to upload all the packaging requirements within your additional borrowing dashboard. During this time, your customer's product will be secure. However, if you do not upload all documents within this timeframe, the application will lapse and you will be required to reapply and rates may have changed.

Need to return to your application to upload packaging documents? Follow these steps:

  • Go to the Broker Portal and select the ‘Existing Customer Portal’ tile.
  • Please Note: Using ‘Case Tracking’ doesn’t support document uploads for additional borrowing applications.
  • Search for your customer using their details.
  • On the Mortgage Summary page, select ‘Additional Borrowing’ at the top.
  • You’ll be taken to the document upload section to complete the packaging.

Note - Only the broker with access to our existing customer platform will be able to upload documents. Our tracking service will become available to administrators access once all packaging has been uploaded and we have successfully recieved the application.

1. Once all packaging tasks have been completed on your application dashboard, we will then conduct a hard credit search on the customer(s) and the application will submit to our underwriters for assessment. We will send you an acknowledgement email to confirm we have recieved the application. 

2. If you have chosen a fee payment product you will then be able to pay any fees that are associated with the application via the additional borrowing dashboard. We will also email you when this task is available to complete.

3. Where a case is agreed by our underwriter, an offer for the additional borrowing amount will then be emailed to your customer(s) which they will need to electronically sign to accept. You will also receive a copy of this document.

4. Once accepted, the funds will be available for release to the customer within 48 hours or they can specify a date after the 48 hour window.

5. The Additional Borrowing sub-account will go live once the funds have been released.

 

If the application being completed includes a product transfer for any existing sub accounts:

6. Once the funds have been released, we will then send an email to the customer(s) notifying them that their Product transfer offer (variation agreement) is ready to accept with instructions on how to confirm their agreement via 'Manage your Mortgage'.

7. After the acceptance has been received, the new rate will take effect either when the existing rate expires, or as soon as possible if they are on SVR or switching as part of the Track & Switch feature. Where there is more than one sub-account switching with different product expiry dates, these will be keyed as separate applications. We’ll send the paperwork for the sub-account that is due to expire first and once the rate is live on that relevant sub-account, we’ll then send the paperwork for the next sub-account(s).

Note - The customer will still only make one monthly repayment by Direct Debit and we will write to advise them how much their new combined monthly payment is and when it will be due.

The Additional Borrowing will be keyed first and only once funds have been released, will the Product Transfer application be processed.

Amending your application

Up until submitting and locking in your application, you can return to the application at any time to make amendments. Information will be stored for upto 180 days unless an application is locked.

After submission and application lock, if you'd like to change the rate or any details of your additional borrowing application this can be done but only after your application has been fully submitted and all document tasks completed.

Once submitted, you can request this change through the application dashboard anytime until offer acceptance. 

Packaging requirements

Our Packaging Requirements for additional borrowing are the same as those for new business applications. 

Where additional borrowing is being raised for home improvements we may require additional information. Please see Home Improvements on our A-Z.

Valuations

We’ll use a variety of valuation tools to get an up-to-date valuation.

  • For loans over 350k or above 60% (or if the property is a flat) - If we need to instruct a valuation this will be at the bank’s expense.
  • For loans under £350,000 and below 60% LTV - We’ll use the House Price Index valuation as long as the property is not a flat.

Procuration fees

You’ll be paid a procuration fee for additional borrowing. 

The additional borrowing and the product transfer will be processed separately. 

The procuration fee (minimum £250) for the additional borrowing will be paid first, once the funds are released. 

Please see Procuration Fees.

FAQs

What rate will the additional borrowing need to be taken on?

'If applying for additional borrowing with a product transfer - The additional borrowing will go on the same rate as the Product Transfer, provided the customer signs and returns the Product Transfer offer (variation agreement) within 14 days. 

If applying for standalone additional borrowing - the rates available will be those for a standalone additional borrowing application.

Can a customer take out additional borrowing on behalf of any joint party?

No, when the application has been assessed and a formal offer is distributed, both customers will receive this offer a copy to sign and both will need to complete in order to complete and release the funds.

Can I save the application part way through and come back to complete at a later stage?

Yes, when you complete a page the information entered is autosaved for up to 180 days so you can pause and restart an application. 

Note - Starting an application doesn’t lock in the rate. To do this you will need to have taken the application past a decision in principle and submitted to 'lock' the application. This will allow the application to hold the product selected for a period of 21 days to allow you to complete the document tasks requested.

Can I amend my customers personal details within the application?

Yes, you are able to update their personal details within the 'Your customer's details' screen.

Please Note: You will be unable to change the customer's title within the application at this time.

Identification documents may be requested and will show within the Document upload facility. See our Identification criteria for more information.

Where will the funds be sent to when they are released?

Funds will be sent to the same bank account that the customer makes their mortgage repayments from.

The only exception is where money is being used to repay a subsequent charge. In this instance the money will be sent directly to the charge holder or solicitor acting on their behalf.

FAQs continued

Can my customer apply for additional borrowing if they have consent to let in place?

Yes. However, where consent to let is in place, additional borrowing cannot be raised for: 

  • Gambling.

  • Any form of business purpose (i.e. start up for a new business).

  • Unsecured Debt Consolidation.

My customer has a Buy to Let mortgage and wants to do additional borrowing. Can they do this?

Yes, your customer will need to do this directly. They can call the existing customer team direct on: 0345 302 0190 (UK Relay 18001 0345 302 0190).

Can additional borrowing be applied for where the customer is on a Help to Buy mortgage?

Help to Buy Shared Equity Scheme

Yes. If the Help to Buy Loan is not being repaid with the additional borrowing, the customer will need to contact the Help to Buy Loan provider to obtain a consent letter for the application. The consent letter will be requested as part of the packaging requirements.

If a solicitor is required, the customer must appoint one at their own expense. An underwriter will request solicitor details following application submission. Any funds used to repay the Help to Buy loan will be sent directly to the solicitor to facilitate removal of the charge. We do not provide solicitor services for this process.

Mortgage Guarantee Scheme 2021-2022, MI Home & New Buy Schemes

We are unable to support additional borrowing applications for the first 7 years when a customer has either of these schemes in place as per their original mortgage offer. You should ask your customer to check their original mortgage offer to see if this impacts them. 

If the additional borrowing has been drawn down, however the product transfer has not yet taken effect, can my customer change the rate?

If you want to change the deal your customer has for their product transfer after the additional borrowing has drawndown, you can do this by logging on to the product transfer portal. You’ll see what deals they’re now eligible for and can choose from these. If a product fee is applicable on the new product, they’ll have to pay this.

My customer wishes to cancel the additional borrowing and progress with the product transfer only, what are my options?

If your customer decides to cancel the additional borrowing application but wishes to continue with the product transfer, they must apply for a new product transfer application based on the rates applicable at the time they apply.

If the additional borrowing application has progressed beyond the completion of all document tasks and is now with underwriters, you will need to speak to our Business Development Team to have this application cancelled before you apply for the product transfer. Please call us on 0345 026 0008 to discuss this.

If you have submitted your application but have not yet completed all the document tasks, note that the application has not yet been hardscored or submitted for assessment. The application can be left for the 21 days to lapse, after which time the application will reset. If your customer wishes to proceed with a product transfer application, they can apply as normal.

FAQs continued

Can my customer have a different term for their additional borrowing than the term on the existing mortgage?

Yes. You can select a different term for the additional borrowing within our term and age requirements. You cannot change the term on the existing borrowing. The customer would have to contact us directly to do this. 

When will I get a mortgage illustration for an additional borrowing with a product transfer application?

When keying the application 2 mortgage illustrations are produced after the product selection page, one is for the product transfer & the other one is for the additional borrowing.

Can my customer apply for additional borrowing if they have Cost of Living Support in place?

If the cutomer has taken out Cost of Living (COL) Support within the last 6 months, they will be unable to apply for additional borrowing. This is due to conditions of the support which means they aren’t eligible to apply for additional borrowing during the 6-month support period – even if they choose to end the support early.

This condition only applies when the customer has taken a temporary switch to Interest Only as part of their COL support. This doesn’t apply for term extensions.

Can my customer raise capital for any purpose when it is Shared Ownership / Co-ownership?

Customers can raise additional funds for buying a larger share in the property (Staircasing) only.
