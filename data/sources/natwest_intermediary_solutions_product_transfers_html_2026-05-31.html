Why choose us?

You can secure a new rate for your customer 4 months before their current deal ends.

Quick, straight forward processing. There is no additional underwriting for standalone product transfers.

Ability to change the product before completion.

Customers can apply for Additional Borrowing at the same time (subject to underwriting).

Product transfer eligibility

Available for residential and Buy to Let customers who are:

  • Within their roll-off period. (4 months before their current deal ends).
  • On a standard variable rate (SVR).
  • Within the track and switch eligibility period on a tracker rate. 
  • The minimum loan amount to secure a new rate is £10,000.
  • More than 27 months term remaining at the date of applying for their product switch (end date of product must be before end date of mortgage).

Customers will need to contact us directly where they: 

  • Want to exit an existing deal ahead of the roll-off period. 
  • Want to change their mortgage term or repayment type.
  • Are in mortgage arrears.
  • Have more than 5 eligible to switch sub accounts.

Application process

  1. Log in to our website.
  2. Enter the customer’s details: Mortgage Account number, Surname, Property Postcode and Date of Birth.
  3. The HPI and Current Valuation figures are available on screen and the higher of these figures is used to calculate the LTV. If the valuation figure is not showing then you must call us to get this updated.
  4. If the customer is eligible to switch, you will be able to choose a new deal from the rates available which will be displayed on the screen.
  5. Once you have finished the application, the next steps will be displayed on the screen. In some circumstances, documents may take up to five working days to be emailed over to the customer and yourself.  If this is the case then the on screen guidance will tell you this.
  6. Customers must accept the offer within 14 calendar days, or the system will automatically cancel their application.
  7. Customer’s need to be registered for online banking to have access to enhanced security and all of the additional services available for our mortgage customers. To accept the rate, they need to log in to Manage your Mortgage via the mobile app or online banking, they will be emailed the steps when their offer is ready to accept. If your customer needs further support in how to register for online banking or how to accept the rate if they are already registered, please use the relevant support pack available below, which you can download and forward directly to your customer.
  8. Once the acceptance has been received, the new rate will take effect when the existing fixed rate expires. Where the existing rate has already expired or where a customer is switching to a fixed rate as part of a track and switch application, the new rate will start as soon as possible after the signed acceptance has been received.
  9. Where there is more than one sub-account that you are looking to switch and they have different product expiry dates, you will submit these as one application. Internally we will then manually key these as separate applications. This means we will send the paperwork for the sub account that is due to expire first and once the rate is live on that sub-account, we will then send the paperwork for the next sub account(s). You do not need to submit any further applications for the eligible sub accounts you have selected.
  10. Support packs available to aid with acceptance if necessary:
  • Registering for online banking
  • Registering for the mobile app
  • Accepting via online banking
  • Accepting via mobile app

For customers to access further Support they can visit our Login help page. Here they will find Troubleshooting & FAQs such as  “I've forgotten my Online Banking login details, what should I do?”

Valuations and procurement fees

Valuations

  • When you log in to apply for a product transfer, you will see the current valuation and also the HPI.
  • We will use the higher of the valuation or the HPI to calculate the Loan to Value.
  • Customers can request a standard valuation where they choose to do so. There is a fee applicable for this. For costs please see ‘valuation fees’ on our property and valuation hub.  The customer will need to call 0345 302 0190 (Relay UK: 18001 0345 302 0190) to arrange this.
  • If a standard valuation is required, this can add additional time to the process. The new valuation will replace the existing valuation, even if it is lower. Once the valuation has been carried out and updated you can then apply for the product transfer application in the portal.

Procurement fees

  • Product Transfer: 0.20% gross (both DA and AR firms).

FAQs

FAQs relating to the product transfer process:

How can the customer pay the product fee?

The product fee can either be:

  • Paid up front: when the customer logs on to accept the deal the system will then take them to our payments screen.
  • Added to the loan: Interest will be payable on the fee along with the loan.

If my customer has more than one sub account on their mortgage, will they pay more than one product fee?

The product fee is payable per product/application.

Where a customer currently has consent to let on their residential mortgage, can they complete a product transfer application?

Yes they can, standard residential products will be displayed.

My customer has not accepted the offer within 14 days, what should I do?

You’ll need to resubmit a new product transfer application based on the rates available at that time.

Can a Product Transfer application be cancelled?

You can cancel a Product Transfer application at any time before the new rate begins.

In the Product Transfer Portal, you’ll have the option to view other deals your customer is eligible for on that day, before cancelling an application.

For fully digital applications, you can cancel the deal in the online portal and you’ll be able to reselect a new deal right away.

There’s a small number of circumstances where you can’t cancel and reapply from within the portal:

•          Where the application has not been fully digital you’ll have the option to select a new deal on our online portal, we’ll then cancel the previous deal for you and send the offer out for the new product you’ve selected.

•          There may also be a small number of occasions where you can’t cancel the rate online. When this is the case, we’ll let you know within the system to call our processing team on 0345 026 0008.

When you cancel a rate with our processing team, it may take 1-2 working days for you to be able to key a new rate on the system. We will notify you when this has been done and you can key a new application based on the rates available at the time of the new application submission. 

If the customer paid a product fee upfront, it could take up to 5 working days for the fee to be fully refunded. You don’t have to wait for the fee to have been received by the customer prior to submitting a new application.

FAQs continued

FAQs relating to the product transfer process:

Is the minimum loan size based on the total loan or each sub-account?

The minimum balance to be switched is £10,000. Please see example:

How can I obtain an Mortgage Illustration/ESIS?

The ESIS will be emailed to you along with the variation agreement once you have submitted an application. 

If you have applied for multiple sub accounts where the current products have different expiry dates, you will get the ESIS & variation agreement for each additional sub account after the earlier sub account is live.

I can’t find your rates on sourcing systems or your website. How do I find them?

To find out what rates are available to your customer, you can log in to our portal using your customers details, and we’ll show what deal's they’ll be eligible for today.

Can I see if my customer has signed the Product Transfer paperwork?

You’ll receive an email confirmation when your customer signs. This information does not appear in Case Tracking or on the Existing Customers Portal

Where can I check if a Product Transfer has completed?

You can check completion status in Case Tracking within Submitted Cases. If the status shows ‘Completion’, the Product Transfer has gone live.

FAQs - additional services

What are the additional services available to mortgage customers through Online or Mobile banking?

Security

Our mobile app and online banking services provide enhanced security needed to access the confidential information in Manage Your Mortgage. Customers can securely log in using facial recognition or biometrics depending on their device. Independent security experts regularly test, update & confirm that our systems meet the highest standards of security.

Climate

We are moving towards all customer communications being issued digitally to help support the Bank’s climate goal and help customers transition to net zero.

Services

Customers can also make one off or regular overpayments, change their direct debt date or the bank account the mortgage is paid from, amend their mortgage term, update "banking my way", obtain a redemption statement, obtain consent to let on the mortgage plus more.

Additionally online banking or mobile banking also gives customers the facility to find out what their credit score is and learn more about their report by using the “Know your credit score" facility which can support you with future mortgage applications.

Without online banking customers will be restricted to the services available to them.

The line is open Monday – Friday, 9am to 5pm.​
