We’re here to support you to help your customers wanting to remortgage

Looking for new ways to give your customers more choice and flexibility with their mortgages? NatWest’s remortgage options could make it easy to offer something extra.

Why choose us?

Free valuations as standard.

Free standard legals or cashback options.

Product options:

  • Valuation fee paid for properties valued up to £10 million AND
  • £350 cashback option OR
  • Standard legal fees paid up to £2m loan size  - includes unencumbered properties. 
    We’ll cover standard remortgage legal fees, with any additional Solicitors' fees being met by the customer. 

Additional borrowing

Additional borrowing is available when your customer remortgages.  We can do additional borrowing up to 90% LTV (Restricted to 80% LTV where there is Debt Consolidation)

Criteria

  • We lend up to age 75 and up to a maximum term of 40 years.

  • Income - We’ll use multiple income streams up to 100%, including second jobs. Have a look at our Income and Packaging guide for acceptable income.

  • Credit check - Soft credit check at agreement in pricinple to allow you to assess credit worthiness.

  • Our Affordability Calculator will give you an accurate reflection of what your customer can borrow with enhanced affordability on a five year product.

Help to Buy remortgages

We can help with  Help to Buy remortgages where the loan remains or where this is being repaid in full.

Consent to Let

We can help with Day one Consent to Let for Armed forces remortgaging from another lender.

What is a remortgage?

A remortgage is a transfer of an existing mortgage from one lender to another, where there is no house move involved. This also applies to properties with no existing mortgage (known as an unencumbered property). Customers can do additional borrowing when they remortgage with us. 

Remortgage criteria

Back-to-Back / Day-1 Remortgages

We can support remortgage applications where the customer has owned their property for less than six months, in eligible scenarios. This flexibility helps you place cases where timelines or circumstances fall outside traditional ownership patterns – see our Day-1 remortgage guidance for more information.

Completion Tolerance

£1,000 Balance Tolerance

To help avoid last-minute delays, we allow a tolerance of up to £1,000 where the final redemption figure differs from the loan amount requested at completion.‑minute delays, we allow a tolerance of up to £1,000 where the final redemption figure differs from the loan amount requested at completion.

As long as the LTV remains within the selected product’s LTV limit and does not increase into a higher LTV band, the case proceeds without re‑underwriting, additional checks, or broker action.

How it works

  • Differences up to £1,000 are handled directly by the solicitor
  • The solicitor requests the tolerance where needed
  • Your original advice remains valid, helping avoid unexpected changes

This tolerance provides reassurance that small redemption variances won’t disrupt your customer’s completion.

Let-to-Buy

Let-to-Buy Remortgages

We can support customers who want to remortgage their existing home onto a Buy-to-Let while moving into a new property – see our Let-to-Buy guidance for more information.

Getting the best deal for your customers

  • Competitive rates: We offer competitive remortgage rates, helping you secure the best deal for your customers.
  • Added value: Customers can benefit from free legals or cashback options, providing immediate savings.
  • Green incentives: Discounted rates are available for homes with an EPC rating of A or B.
  • Overpayment flexibility: Your customers can overpay up to 20% of their mortgage each year without penalties, helping them reduce overall interest and pay off their loan sooner.

Help your customers manage their money

  • Interest Only mortgages: We offer interest only options (up to 80% LTV and subject to eligibility), allowing customers to lower their monthly outgoings while making the most of their property.
  • Term adjustment: Customers can extend or reduce their mortgage term whilst remortgaging. They can tailor repayments to their changing circumstance and either lower payments or pay off their mortgage faster.
  • Debt consolidation: Simplify your customers’ monthly payments by consolidating their debts when they remortgage to NatWest.

Help your customers achieve their home goals

  • Borrow more when needed: If your customers need additional funds for home improvements or changing life situations, they can apply to borrow more at the same rate during their NatWest remortgage.
  • Porting options: For customers looking for their next move, they can port their mortgage at the same rate should they decide to move home, offering flexibility and peace of mind.
  • Flexible repayment options: Customers can switch to an interest only mortgage now or later (subject to eligibility) and make home improvements with additional borrowing as their needs evolve.

Let NatWest help you deliver real value, flexibility, and support to your customers - today and for the future.
